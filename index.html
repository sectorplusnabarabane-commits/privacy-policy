Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Angel of Heaven mobile application (the "Application") created by SECTORplus.


Information Collection and Use

The Application may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data
  • Advertising ID
  • Anonymous Player ID provided by Unity Authentication
  • Gameplay statistics, including scores, leaderboard data, and player display names used in online features
  • Online multiplayer service data, including lobby, matchmaking, and relay connection data where applicable

This data is used for the following purposes:

  • Displaying advertisements
  • Processing in-app purchases
  • Improving application performance
  • Providing online gameplay, leaderboards, matchmaking, and multiplayer connectivity

The Application does not require account registration and does not collect precise location data.


Advertising

The Application uses third-party advertising services such as Unity Ads or Google AdMob. These services may collect and process data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.


In-App Purchases

The Application offers in-app purchases. Payments are securely processed by Google Play. The Application does not collect or store payment details.


Online Services

The Application may use Unity Gaming Services, including Authentication, Leaderboards, Lobby, Relay, and other related online systems, to provide online gameplay features.

These services may process data such as anonymous Player ID, leaderboard scores, player display names, matchmaking data, lobby participation data, relay connection information, and gameplay-related statistics required for online functionality.

The Application uses third-party services that may collect and share data:

These services may collect:

Data Sharing

The Application may share collected data with third-party services for advertising, analytics, payment processing, anonymous authentication, leaderboard functionality, matchmaking, lobby management, and multiplayer networking.
